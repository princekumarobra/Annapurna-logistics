Company Profile

Manpower services & logistics support based in Seraikella, Jharkhand.

About Us

Annapurna Logistics was established in 2024 with the aim of providing reliable and cost-effective manpower and logistics solutions. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we have built a reputation for timely delivery, efficient workforce management, and transparent operations.

Our dedicated team ensures that every client receives the best possible service, tailored to their specific needs.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become a trusted partner in the logistics and manpower service industry by continuously improving our service standards, embracing innovation, and maintaining long-term client relationships.

Mission

To deliver efficient, professional, and sustainable manpower and logistics services that help businesses grow and operate smoothly.

Key Services

  • Manpower supply services for warehouses, factories & logistics hubs.
  • Loading & unloading operations.
  • Parcel & goods transport coordination.
  • Route planning, scheduling & documentation support.
  • Workforce management & supervision.
